Growth Strategy · Traffic & Leads

One brand. Four funnels. The growth is in not selling them the same way.

Vanspeed is a premium Sprinter conversion brand that builds in Westminster and ships nationwide. A six-figure build buyer and a parts buyer are both on the site. The plan separates those funnels, fixes the measurement underneath them, and turns the demand already searching into booked consults.

Prepared by Tirso Subject vanspeedshop.com Date June 2026
01

How I read the business

One brand is quietly running four businesses, and each one is bought differently. Most of the strategy comes down to respecting that.

Custom Build

The flagship

Six-figure ticket, 6 to 8 week wait. High-ticket lead gen. Nobody buys on the first visit.

Ready-Built

In stock, with urgency

Same money, but available now. The pitch writes itself: skip the wait, drive it home.

Parts & Apparel

Ecommerce

Lower ticket, repeat buyers, and it reaches DIY owners who will never buy a full build.

Rental · AltCamp

Try before you buy

A quiet on-ramp. A rental can turn straight into a build sale.

A $180K build buyer and a $300 roof-rack buyer are both here. Market to them the same way and you waste budget on both.

02

The foundation: data, tracking, reporting

Low volume, high ticket. You win on a handful of big sales, so the whole thing breaks if you measure the wrong signal. This is the cheapest, highest-return work, and everything else rides on it.

GTM

Tagging done on purpose

Clean dataLayer and real events per funnel: quiz start, quiz complete, consult request, layout and build page views, add to cart, Ready-Built view. Consent Mode v2 so we stay compliant and keep modeled conversions instead of throwing the data away.

HubSpot

Close the loop

HubSpot is already in place. Put the consult pipeline on real deal stages, then feed outcomes back to Google and Meta as offline conversions. The platforms optimize toward deposits and sales, not form fills.

Breeze · Reverse IP

De-anonymize the traffic

Most people researching a six-figure van never fill out a form. Breeze Intelligence identifies and enriches them by reverse IP. Sales gets a warm list of high-intent visitors who never raised a hand, and forms get shorter because Breeze backfills the data. Less friction, more conversions.

Call Tracking

Catch the phone buyers

On a six-figure purchase, people call. Untracked calls make the channels that actually sell look dead.

Reporting

One view that drives decisions

A single Looker Studio dashboard over GA4, ad spend, and HubSpot pipeline. Weekly read for optimization, monthly for strategy, lead scoring underneath. If a number does not change a decision, it does not go on the board.

03

The channels

Search, SEO & AEO

Paid search split by funnel plus brand defense so nobody bids on the Vanspeed name. SEO targets the research queries buyers hit early: what a conversion costs, 144 versus 170, is van life worth it, plus region pages and the "best builder" roundups. AEO on top: structure pages with clear question-led answers and FAQ schema, keep the brand entity consistent, and earn mentions in the sources AI engines quote, so Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT and Perplexity name Vanspeed when someone asks who builds the best Sprinter.

Lifecycle in HubSpot

The buying window runs 6 to 12 months, so nurture matters. All of it lives in HubSpot: a build track (owner stories, financing, new inventory) and the parts flows that are missing today (post-quiz welcome, cart abandonment, inventory alerts, post-handover referral ask). Rental customers get their own track, since someone who already slept in a Vanspeed is the warmest build lead there is.

Conversion & speed to lead

The "Start Your Journey" quiz is the best hook on the site. Cut its friction, let Breeze keep forms short, and add a financing calculator since financing takes the fear out of a big buy. Then wire HubSpot to route and notify the second a lead lands, with an SMS follow-up. Leads go cold in minutes.

05

Where the upside is

  • Existing content and real build expertise. Structured into SEO and AEO pages, it ranks and gets cited by AI engines at almost no media cost.
  • Parts as a cheap way into the brand. A small accessory buyer comes back for a van or sends a friend.
  • Measurement plus Breeze. What makes a low-conversion business scalable, and it surfaces demand that is invisible today.
  • Financing as a conversion lever. It moves more buyers off the fence than any new creative.
